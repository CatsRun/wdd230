Scoots: Scooter Rentals

Come ride with us on an adventure through ocean waves and beautiful trees on the tropical island of Cozumel. Located in the western Caribbean Sea, off the coast of Mexico, this picturesque retreat will give you everything you dream of. With two convenient locations we are here to provide you with the freedom to explore. We are located near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast. We rent motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs.
